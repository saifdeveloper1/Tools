How to Optimize Your Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust settings using the quality and compression level controls
  3. Click "Compress Image" to process your file (takes just seconds)
  4. Download the optimized version and use it on your website or app

Why Optimize Images?

  • Faster page loads - Compressed images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Better SEO - Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Reduced bandwidth - Save on hosting costs and data usage
  • Improved conversions - Faster sites have lower bounce rates

Supported Formats

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and next-generation WEBP format which provides superior compression.
